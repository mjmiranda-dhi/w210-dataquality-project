So, how does all this work, anyway?

This didn't come about overnight.  We did build this in only twelve weeks, though, and we were able to do it because we used the skills and education we gained in the UC Berkeley MIDS program.  

Our goal with ERRATUM.IO isn't to build the greatest whizbang widget you've ever seen -- rather, we want to bring a little of the power of data science straight to you.  Sometimes it's a really good idea to let the experts handle the details, whether they fix your car, give you legal or medical advice, handle your taxes, or help you fix your data.   That way, you can spend your time doing what's really important: using your data.

If you aren't sure how our process can help you scrub data, check out this section of our site on hierarchical data and the idea of master data.

Below are a few things that we've incorporated behind the scenes to make this whole thing work.  Thank you to all the great people who build and maintain this fantastic software.
